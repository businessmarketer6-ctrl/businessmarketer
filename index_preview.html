Most searches, video views, and purchases here happen on a smartphone — often over patchy networks and in regional languages. A thoughtful mobile marketing strategy in India respects those realities instead of copying Western playbooks.

  • ✓Vernacular creatives — Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Marathi lift engagement.

Questions, answered

Frequently asked questions

How much does it cost to hire a digital marketing agency in India? +

It varies with scope and goals. Retainers range from a modest monthly fee for a single service to larger budgets for full-funnel growth, with ad spend usually separate from agency fees. The right question isn't "how cheap" but "what return" — a good agency should pay for itself.

How long before I see results? +

Paid channels like Google Ads and Meta Ads can generate leads within days to a few weeks. SEO and organic social are longer plays — usually three to six months for meaningful momentum — but they compound and lower your cost per lead over time.

Do I need both Google Ads and Meta Ads? +

For most growing businesses, yes. A Google Ads agency in India captures people actively searching, while a meta ads agency in India builds awareness and retargets. Together they cover the full buying journey.

Is mobile marketing really that important in India? +

Absolutely. With the majority of users browsing and buying on smartphones, a mobile marketing strategy in India is the foundation of how customers discover and trust your brand — not an optional add-on.

Can a single agency handle every channel well? +

The strongest agencies do, because integrated strategy outperforms scattered efforts. Look for proven depth across SEO, paid media, and social media marketing in India rather than a one-trick specialist.
